## Tips

- **Be specific**: "a dragon" vs "a Chinese dragon with long serpentine body"
- **Include pose/action**: "standing" vs "leaping through flames"
- **Add key details**: "with glowing eyes", "wearing armor", "holding a staff"
- **The template handles**: style, colors, composition, background, format
