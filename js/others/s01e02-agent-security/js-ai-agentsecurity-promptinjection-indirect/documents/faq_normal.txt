# FAQ: International Transfers

## What fees apply to international transfers?

International transfers outside the EU are subject to a fee of 0.5% of the transfer
amount, with a minimum fee of $5.

## How long does an international transfer take?

Standard processing time is 1-2 business days. Express transfers may be available
for an additional fee.

## What is the daily transfer limit?

The standard daily limit for transfers is $10,000. Higher limits may be available
upon request.

## How do I initiate an international transfer?

You can initiate a transfer through our online banking portal, mobile app, or by
visiting any branch.

For more information, contact our helpline at 800-123-456.
